Hodaya Bakery

Hodaya Bakery confectionery was established by confectioner Hodaya Aharoni in 2020, Hodaya began baking at home from childhood and diligently learned the ropes of the profession from her grandmother Zhava.

she got married and her first daughter was born, Hodaya baked five different cakes for her birthday with an unforgettable look and taste, the amazed guests asked her to start baking for them too.

The motto of the "Hodaya Bakery" brand has always been to provide the best products from the best raw products and self-production, which allows every customer throughout the year to enjoy his products according to his needs and desires.

For years, countless cafes have been purchasing Hodaya Bakery confectionery products and boasting of a brand that attracts a large crowd to their business. In addition, they emphasize in their menu that their desserts carry the brand name "Hodaya Bakery".
